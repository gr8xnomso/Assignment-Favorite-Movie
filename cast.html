A List of Five Fauda Cast And a Short Bio About them

Itzik Cohen

Itzik Cohen is internationally recognized for his powerful portrayal of Captain Gabi Ayub, head of the interrogations department at the Shin Bet, in the hit series Fauda.

Career Highlights

  • Early Career: After serving in the Israel Defense Forces, Cohen earned a BA in Theater from Tel Aviv University in 1993.
  • Diverse Background: He co-founded the pioneering drag band Bnot Pesia in 1996, which helped bring drag culture into the Israeli mainstream.
  • Stage and Screen: He has had a prolific career in theater, starring in productions like The Producers, Hairspray (as Edna Turnblad), and Fiddler on the Roof.
  • Notable Films:He gained critical acclaim for the 2009 film A Matter of Size, where his performance as a sumo wrestler earned him a Best Actor award and an Ophir nomination.
  • Recent Work: Beyond Fauda, he has appeared in The Beauty Queen of Jerusalem, The Women's Balcony, and Maktub.

Personal Life

Born in Tel Aviv-Yafo, Cohen has two children. In 2013, he underwent a significant physical transformation after bariatric surgery, losing approximately 50 kg.


Netta Garti

Netta Garti (also spelled Neta Garty) is an acclaimed Israeli actress best known to international audiences for her role as Gali Kavillio in the political thriller series Fauda.

Short Bio
Born:March 20, 1980, in Tel Aviv, Israel.

Role in Fauda:Garti plays Gali, the wife of undercover counterterrorism officer Doron Kavillio (played by Lior Raz). Her character is characterized by her struggle with the danger and instability of her husband’s profession.

Career Highlights:

Before Fauda, she gained recognition for her role as Nicole Shushan in the 2004 film Turn Left at the End of the World (earning an Israeli Film Academy nomination) and as Young Rachel Brener in the 2007 film The Debt. She also appeared as Cookie in the Israeli series The Arbitrator.

Background: She is from a Bulgarian Jewish family and has extensive experience in theatre with the Cameri Theatre in Tel Aviv.

Personal Life:She has been married to Israeli musician Peter Roth since 2007.

Rona-Lee Shim'on

(born January 9, 1983) is an Israeli actress, dancer, and model, best known internationally for her leading role as Nurit in the critically acclaimed Netflix political-thriller series Fauda.

Short Bio

Early Life & Dance: Born in Ramat Gan, Israel, to a Polish, Iraqi, and Persian Jewish family, she began ballet training at age 3 and later joined the renowned Bat Dor dance troupe. She trained at the Royal Ballet Academy in Amsterdam on a full scholarship before returning to Israel to dance professionally.

Transition to Acting: She transitioned to acting in her early 20s, featuring in Israeli television and film, including Our Song and Policeman (2011).

Fauda Role: As Nurit in Fauda (2015–2022), she plays the only woman in an elite, male-dominated undercover counter-terrorism unit. She performed her own stunts and learned Krav Maga for the role.

Other Roles: She appeared in the Netflix series Messiah (2020) as Mika, and in the Amazon series The Terminal List: Dark Wolf (2025) as Mossad agent Eliza Perash.

Personal: She is known for her intense training, including gun handling taught by her brother, Almog Shimon.

Key Details

  • Born: January 9, 1983, in Ramat Gan, Israel
  • Known For: Fauda (Nurit), Messiah (Mika), The Terminal List
  • Skills:Krav Maga, Ballet, Kickboxing

Doron Ben-David

(born September 30, 1980) is an Israeli actor best known for his role as Hertzel "Steve" Pinto in the hit Israeli television series Fauda (2015–present).

Short Bio

  • Role in Fauda: Ben-David plays Steve, a dedicated member of the Mista'arvim (undercover) unit, appearing as a series regular alongside Lior Raz throughout the show's run.
  • Early Life & Education: Born in Tel Aviv, Ben-David was raised in Bat Yam and served in the Israel Defense Forces (IDF) as an inspector at the Erez Crossing. He later studied acting at the Beit Zvi performing arts school.
  • Career & Other Projects: Beyond Fauda, he is known for his roles in the Israeli police drama Manayek (as Ofir Leibowitz) and the 2022 drama series The Lesson. He is also a theater actor and an acting coach.
  • Personal Life: He is married to Mittal, a yoga instructor, and they have three children.

Hisham Suliman

(born February 28, 1978) is an Arab-Israeli actor and director known for his pivotal role as Taufiq Hammed—nicknamed "The Panther"—in the first season of the acclaimed Netflix political thriller series Fauda.

Bio

Born and raised in Nazareth, Israel, to a Palestinian Arab-Muslim family, Suliman began acting in community theatre as a child. He graduated from the Yoram Loewenstein Performing Arts Studio in Tel Aviv in 2000.

Career

He gained international recognition for his role in Steven Spielberg's Munich (2005) and for his role in the 2013 drama Bethlehem. In addition to Fauda, he has appeared in Homeland, The Beauty Queen of Jerusalem, and One Day in October (2024–2025).

Personal Life

He is married to actress Rahik Haj Yahi, and they live in Nof Hagalil, Israel, with their three children.

Fauda Role

As "The Panther" in Fauda, Suliman brought depth and complexity to the character of a high-ranking Hamas militant who was believed to be dead, driving the conflict in the show's intense first season.
